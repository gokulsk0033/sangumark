5. Bone Health: Groundnut oil contains essential minerals like calcium and magnesium, which are vital for maintaining strong and healthy bones.
6. Digestive Health: The healthy fats in groundnut oil can aid in digestion and may help relieve constipation when consumed in moderation.
7. Nutrient Absorption: Groundnut oil enhances the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, and K) from the foods you eat, optimizing nutrient intake.
8. Energy Source: The fats in groundnut oil provide a quick and sustainable source of energy for the body.
9. Skin Nourishment: Vitamin E in groundnut oil nourishes the skin, promoting a healthy complexion and helping to protect against UV damage.
10. Immune Support: The antioxidants present in groundnut oil may support the immune system, helping the body defend against infections and diseases.
