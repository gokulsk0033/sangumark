About us

At Sri Ram Oil Mill, we take immense pride in providing you with the finest selection of edible oils that not only enhance the flavors of your culinary creations but also promote a healthier lifestyle. Since our establishment in 2000, we have garnered years of expertise and a commitment to excellence, making us a leading name in the industry, trusted by countless customers.

Our promise

Quality First: We believe that the foundation of a great product starts with the highest quality ingredients. That's why, for over two decades, we have manufactured our edible oils from high quality seeds, ensuring that every bottle meets the most stringent quality standards.

Health and nutrition

Your well-being is our priority. For more than 20 years, our range of edible oils is carefully selected to cater to your specific dietary needs, whether you're looking for heart-healthy options or plant-based alternatives. We strive to offer oils that contribute to a balanced and nutritious diet.
